CUSTOMIZATION

Our customization technology enables instant change of shape, size, laminate, color, hardwares, etc of a furtniture. Internal features like shelves, drawer, basket, pullout, etc can be interactively fine tuned to a customer’s requirement.

VIRTUALIZATION

Our in-house developed technology that let’s anyone virtualize a bedroom or a kitchen from a set of photographs taken from our app. It automatically constructs a room same size and dimension with doors, windows and tiling placed at right pace.

AUGMENTED REALITY

Our Augmented reality is one of it’s kind. It simply works with any kind of smart card as a marker. Smart card could be a debit card, credit card, driving license, pan card, etc. It’s a great tool to visualize product in a home setup before buying it.
